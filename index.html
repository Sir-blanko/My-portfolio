About Me

I'm a STEM tutor, academic writer and developer based in Kenya. I love turning hard topics into simple explanations and visual stories that actually stick for exams and real life.

My core subjects are Mathematics, Computer Studies, Physics and Geography, and I also help with structured notes, research summaries, lab write‑ups and online coursework support. Every lesson is tailored to the student's syllabus, level and goals.

Beyond teaching, I design digital tools and interfaces that support learning — from question banks to dashboards. This mix of teaching, writing and building lets me give students both clear theory and practical skills.
